About Us

Common diseases are those that affect a large portion of the global population, often chronic and requiring long-term health management. These include conditions

like diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart diseases. Symptoms vary depending on the condition; for example, diabetes may involve increased thirst or fatigue, while asthma and heart diseases may present with shortness of breath or chest pain
